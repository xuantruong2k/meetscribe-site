# Privacy

Privacy policy — coming soon.

In short: MeetScribe is a local-first desktop app. It has no backend. Your audio, transcripts, and summaries stay on your machine. The app talks to the STT and LLM providers *you* configure with *your* API keys — we never see your data or your keys.

This page (the site itself) is static HTML served by Cloudflare Pages. No third-party analytics, no cookies, no tracking pixels.

A full breakdown of every network destination, what is stored locally, and what is never stored anywhere will land here before launch.
